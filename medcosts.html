MedCosts

MedCosts is a web application designed to assist medical professionals at UNC Hospitals.  (Unfortunately, you need a UNC login to get past the authentication page.)

MedCosts allows doctors look up Medicare information wherever, whenever -- built as a mobile-first web application, MedCosts emphasizes agility and ease of use. The app is packed with tools to help doctors keep track of the work they've done, and has a range of customizable setttings so users can quickly access the information related to their particular field.

MedCosts was built with Python's Django framework.
