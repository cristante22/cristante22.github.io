MedCosts

MedCosts is a web application designed to assist medical professionals at UNC Hospitals.
Believe it or not, doctors rarely know how much your medical procedures cost!  Sometimes, patients want to know, especially if they lack insurance coverage.

MedCosts allows doctors to access this information wherever, whenever -- built as a mobile-first web application, MedCosts emphasizes agility and ease of use. The app is packed in with tools to help doctors keep track of the work they've done, and has a range of customizable setttings so users can quickly access the information related to their particular field.
